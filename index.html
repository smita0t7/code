Bangtan synodrome


BTS is one of the most popular South Korean music bands dominated by teenage and young adult boys. The South Korean music band was introduced in 2010, and the first music video titled ‘2 Cool 4 Skool’ was released in 2013 under the production company Big Hit Music. Bangtan Sonyeondan is the full form of BTS. Bangtan Sonyeondan is a combination of South Korean words mean resistance and hindrance to the principles that are a significant part of society. People are expected to accept these principles as something ordained by God. The music band encourages young adults to enquire about the truth, interpret the facts and figures and then decide if he wants to accept the principle or not. All the music videos released by the South Korean boy band revolve around present-day social issues prevailing in a society, for example, increasing levels of anxiety, stress and depression, lack of confidence, self-belief and self-esteem, unacceptance of a particular group in society, and the list goes on. The South Korean boy band is led by 7 young boys who are either in their teenage or in their early 20s. The attitude and courageous lyrics of the boy band earned the music band the title ‘Bulletproof Boys Scout.’ The South Korean music band published the debut album in 2014 under the title ‘Dark and Wild.’ Dark and Wild became extremely popular in South Korea, and the artists amassed appreciation and acknowledgment from the public.



In 2016, the boy band came up with another album named ‘Wings.’ Their single album Wings was published in their country’s national language. BTS is considered the first music band that has become extremely popular with their record-breaking music videos in the United States of America. One of their famous albums, ‘Love Yourself,’ was featured in the US Billboard 2018. Millions of proponents from all over the world shower their relentless support and compassion towards the boy band.
